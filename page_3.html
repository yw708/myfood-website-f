Congee 粥

Congee is a soft rice porridge that is perfect for breakfast or on cold winter days. It’s light, easy to digest, and soothing.

The taste is mild and warm, often flavored with meat, fish, or preserved eggs for extra aroma.

How to Make Congee:

  • 1. Wash 1 cup of rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  • 2. Add rice to 6 cups of water or broth.
  • 3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 1–1.5 hours until creamy.
  • 4. Add ingredients like shredded chicken, fish, or preserved egg if desired.
  • 5. Season with salt and a little sesame oil before serving.
